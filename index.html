      About Finzaro

      Finzaro is a smart, user-friendly Expense Tracker designed to help you take full control of your personal finances. Whether you want to record daily expenses, monitor your income, or calculate your monthly savings, Finzaro makes it quick and simple — no sign-up or internet required.

      This modern financial tracker uses local storage to save your data securely on your own device, so your privacy is always protected. With an intuitive interface and elegant dark/light themes, Finzaro ensures you can manage your money with comfort and clarity anytime, anywhere.

      Finzaro’s mission is to help you spend smarter, save faster, and plan better. Start tracking your expenses today and experience how effortless financial control can be.
